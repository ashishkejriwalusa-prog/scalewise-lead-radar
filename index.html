Boolean Search Guide

Search syntax: Use quotes for exact phrases, uppercase AND/OR/NOT, and parentheses to group options.

Examples

("bookkeeper" OR "bookkeeping") AND ("contract" OR "part-time" OR "1099") NOT (resume OR candidate)
("CPA firm" OR "accounting firm") AND (capacity OR seasonal OR "tax season")
